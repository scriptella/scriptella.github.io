Community Support

For community support, you can ask questions on StackOverflow or visit Scriptella ETL Google Group.

Commercial Support

Scriptella is in maintenance mode and hasn't been actively developed for several years. Due to my primary employment at various tech companies in Silicon Valley, it's very difficult or even impossible to spend time working on open source.

If you are using Scriptella for business or interested in using it in the future, feel free to share your ideas/proposals on the mailing list or drop me a line at scriptella@gmail.com to discuss options for commercial support or any other business ideas. I'm exploring different options that will allow me to focus more on Scriptella ETL development. Starting with evaluation of demand for commercial support and feature development.
