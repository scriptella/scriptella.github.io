Scriptella ETL Project Support

Community Support

For community support, you can ask questions on StackOverflow or visit Scriptella ETL Google Group.

Commercial Support

Scriptella is currently in maintenance mode and has not been actively developed for several years.

If you are using Scriptella for business or are interested in utilizing it in the future, please feel free to share your ideas or proposals on the mailing list. You can also reach out to discuss options for commercial support or any other business inquiries by emailing scriptella@gmail.com. We are evaluating demand for commercial support and feature development to better serve our users.

For performance troubleshooting and optimization, we recommend reaching out to our partners at PVRLabs, experts in Java performance optimization.

Issue Tracker

If you think that you've found a bug or want to propose a feature to implement? Use the GitHub project Issue Tracker.
